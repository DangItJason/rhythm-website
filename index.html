Questions we hear at 11pm

How fast can care actually start?

Usually within 48 hours of your first call: nurse assessment tomorrow, caregiver the day after. After a hospital discharge we can often move faster. Say so when you call.

Will my mom have the same caregiver every visit?

Yes, that's the point of Right-Fit Match. One primary caregiver on a set schedule, plus one familiar backup who has already met your parent, so a sick day never means a stranger.

What if the caregiver doesn't show up?

Every shift has backup coverage built in before it starts. You'd see the change in the app and get a text from us first, not a silent no-show.

Is this medical care?

No. We're a non-medical home care agency: daily living help, meals, rides, medication reminders. A registered nurse assesses every client and reviews the plan monthly, and we coordinate with your parent's doctors when things change.

Is there really no contract or deposit?

Really. You pay $38 an hour for the hours you book, four hours minimum per visit. Pause or stop any time with a week's notice.

My dad says he doesn't want a stranger in the house.

Most of our clients said that too. That's why the nurse visit comes first, why we match by personality, and why it's the same face every week. Strangers don't stay strangers on a schedule.
